Our Story

Since 1892

Founded by industrialist and philanthropist Aldous Aethon, the university was established with a radical belief: that great education is not a privilege — it is the engine of human progress.

Our Mission

To advance knowledge for the benefit of humanity

Over 130 years, Aethon has been shaped by the belief that a great university must do three things: teach rigorously, research fearlessly, and engage the world honestly. These principles remain unchanged.

Today, our 350,000 living alumni lead governments, institutions, companies, and movements on every continent — living proof that an Aethon education is a force multiplier for whatever good you wish to do in the world.
